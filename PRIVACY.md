# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

Interlace Remote does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

The app connects only to the Interlace server URL that you provide. Any media library information, playback state, download links, torrent files, subtitles, or system status shown in the app comes from your own self-hosted Interlace server.

The developer does not operate an Interlace server for you and cannot access your server, media files, downloads, playback activity, or network configuration.

Interlace Remote may use local device storage to remember your server URL and app preferences. This information stays on your device unless you remove it through iOS system settings or uninstall the app.

If you contact the developer for support, the information you choose to include in your message will be used only to respond to your request.

Interlace Remote is not affiliated with or endorsed by Kodi, Team Kodi, XBMC Foundation, aria2, or their respective projects.

For privacy questions, contact the developer through GitHub Issues:
https://github.com/YaphetSf/Interlace/issues
